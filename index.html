Bob's Bullets

Bob has been making cast lead bullets since 2002. We offer a wide assortment of calibers, sizes, and weights. Based in Ohio and founded by accomplished bullseye pistol shooters, Bob's Bullets are used by top shooters throughout the USA and abroad.

Note: We do not have the ability to accept orders online via credit card. Use the information on our shipping page to place an order, or contact us for more information.

Alloy Mix: We use a mix of 92% lead, 6% antimony, and 2% tin. This alloy has a Brinell hardness of approximately 16.
